So... what's a robot? 🤔
A robot is a machine that can do stuff on its own (or almost on its own).

It's basically a really cool machine that thinks a little, moves around, and does tasks.
Kind of like a very obedient pet… but made of metal. 🦾

Sensors = Robot Senses 👀👂
Sensors are how robots feel the world.
A light sensor sees brightness. A sound sensor hears noise.
A touch sensor knows when it bumps into things!
🎮 Challenge: Can you answer a quick question? Tap here! +10 pts
Actuators = Robot Muscles 💪
Actuators are what make robots actually MOVE.
The most common actuator is a motor — it spins really fast
and that spinning can turn wheels, move arms, or even make sounds!
⚡ Motors turn electricity into movement — magic! (okay, it's physics, but still)
The Brain 🧠 (aka the Computer)
The brain is just a tiny computer chip inside the robot.
It reads info from sensors, thinks about it (very fast!),
and tells the motors what to do.

It all works together! 🎉
Robots do this loop millions of times per second:
Sense → Think → Act → Sense → Think → Act...
That's how a robot vacuum doesn't fall down the stairs!
(Most of the time anyway 😅)
